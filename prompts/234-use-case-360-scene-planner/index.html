Full Prompt

Create one polished public-gallery concept image titled exactly "Use Case: 360 Scene Planner".

Output format: one ultra-wide 2:1 equirectangular panorama concept image, full-bleed immersive environment with a thin transparent QA overlay. It must not be a physical tabletop object, not a device, not a tray, not a lab bench, not a map, not a comic, not a worksheet, not a contact sheet, and not a dashboard-first UI. The scene itself should dominate.

Scene: a fictional no-people indoor/outdoor reading courtyard called "Aurora Reading Court". It is a calm circular courtyard at blue hour with curved book alcoves, glass ceiling ribs, paper lanterns, shallow reflecting channel, tiled floor compass motif, and soft plants. The left edge and right edge should visibly match as if the panorama can wrap around seamlessly: the same brass moon gate continues across both edges, with matching lantern height and matching tile pattern. No real architecture, no real brand, no logo.

GPT Image play mechanism shown visually: small overlay chips and hairline guide marks prove the panorama is useful for 360 planning: "Left seam", "Right seam", "Horizon", "Anchor A", "Anchor B", "Anchor C", "Wrap OK", "2:1", "PASS". Add three subtle anchor motifs that repeat consistently: a teal crescent tile, an amber lantern pair, and a graphite floor star. Keep all in-image text short, readable, and exactly as listed; do not add random placeholder text.

Composition: true panorama feeling, horizon centered, continuous perspective, gentle parallax cues, left/right seam windows lightly outlined at the far edges, central quiet reading court in the middle, no people. Use warm amber lantern light, deep teal shadows, graphite linework, ivory signs, restrained coral QA warning marks. Make it visually rich and distinct from AI sports posters or generic dramatic posters.

Safety and rights: fictional location only, no real brands, no trademarks, no public figures, no politics, no elections, no dangerous instructions, no adult or explicit content, no gore or violence, no copyrighted characters, no living-artist style imitation, no source image reuse, no watermark.
