Full Prompt

Create a second polished 3x2 visual research board titled "DERIVED PLAY MODES". Six experimental GPT-image-2 gameplay/use-case concepts, each with a short readable English label and a small Chinese subtitle. Panel 1: "Prompt Escape Room" showing a puzzle poster where hidden visual clues unlock the next prompt. Panel 2: "Brand Multiverse" showing the same fictional tea brand across cyberpunk, museum, and grocery shelf identities. Panel 3: "Before/After Time Machine" showing one street corner across 1926, 2026, and 2126. Panel 4: "UI Fiction Simulator" showing imaginary app screens for an impossible product, realistic but fictional. Panel 5: "Recipe to Comic" showing a cooking recipe transformed into a 4-panel comic with readable step labels. Panel 6: "Worldbuilding Atlas" showing a fantasy transit map, stamps, product labels, and field notes for one fictional city. Contemporary design research board, crisp layout, strong but not chaotic color, readable labels, original fictional content only, no real logos, no celebrity likenesses.
