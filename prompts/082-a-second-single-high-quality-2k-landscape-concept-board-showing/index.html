Full Prompt

Create a second single high-quality 2K landscape concept board showing six derivative game-like GPT Image 2 play formats inspired by the first set. No readable text except simple abstract symbols. Style: playful but polished product concept art, clean panels, energetic interaction, vivid non-monochrome palette, no logos, no famous people, no copyrighted characters.

Six panels:
1. Visual treasure hunt: a generated city scene contains hidden whimsical objects, with magnifying-glass UI overlays and players pointing at clues.
2. Brand remix slot machine: a fictional snack package cycles through packaging styles, photo scenes, and poster formats like a creative slot machine.
3. Moodboard battle cards: two players compare generated fashion/interior mood cards on a tabletop, each card has coherent color/material props.
4. Time-machine photo chain: one family snapshot becomes three versions across eras and genres, shown as a branching timeline wall.
5. Character passport: an original mascot character appears in passport-stamp pages across different worlds while staying visually consistent.
6. Prompt recipe kitchen: ingredients like color chips, camera lenses, layout tiles, and emotion tokens are mixed in a chef-like studio to generate a final poster.

Make it look like a record of product-play experiments: clear visual ideas, each panel self-explanatory, cohesive grid, no exact readable typography.
