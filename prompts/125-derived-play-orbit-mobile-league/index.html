Full Prompt

Create a single high-quality landscape concept image showing an original derived GPT Image 2 play mode called Orbit Mobile League, as a suspended kinetic mobile sculpture game, not a board, not a grid, not cards, not a circular rules machine. The image should feel like a playable tabletop object hanging in space: brass arms, translucent crop frames, magnetic moon tokens, reference-image charms, small mask shutters, and balance weights suspended from thin wires above a clean dark tabletop.

Represent six game mechanics as physical actions on the mobile, integrated into one sculpture: 1) parallax-star-hop, players move a reference charm between depth hooks to test whether the generated scene preserves the intended object; 2) eclipse-token-lock, a moon token can cover exactly one harmless edit area before the next prompt; 3) orbit-window-draft, players draft aspect-ratio frames and must keep the focal subject inside the moving frame; 4) glyph-meteor-race, short invented labels are checked for legibility as they pass through a narrow light beam; 5) nebula-signal-sort, messy prompt fragments are sorted into useful constraints versus vague fog; 6) safe-harbor-swap, any real-looking mark is swapped for a fictional symbol before scoring.

Visual style: polished miniature product photography mixed with refined 3D illustration, clear rule affordances, rich material detail, elegant museum-object presentation. Composition: wide 16:9, shallow perspective, the mobile sculpture occupies the center with visible hanging pieces and a few small score beads, no six boxed panels, no route map, no arcade table, no weather gauges, no loom, no printshop, no film set. Palette: matte black tabletop, warm brass, translucent glass, pearl white, teal shadows, small coral scoring marks. Safety constraints: all symbols are fictional; no real brands, no logos, no celebrity likenesses, no public political figures, no parties or elections, no sexual content, no harmful instructions, no graphic violence, no copyrighted characters, no living-artist style imitation, no watermark.
