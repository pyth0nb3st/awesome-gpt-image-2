Full Prompt

Create a single high-quality 2K landscape concept board showing six NEW derivative game-like play formats for GPT Image 2. Avoid repeating hidden-object treasure hunt, brand slot machine, moodboard battle, time-machine photo chain, character passport, prompt recipe kitchen, toy remixes, micro-brand kits, and old photo repair. No readable text except abstract symbols. Style: polished game/product design artifact, clean six-panel grid, energetic interaction, vivid balanced palette.

Six panels:
1. Regression detective: two near-identical generated scenes side by side with players marking changed details, lighting drift, missing props, and style inconsistencies.
2. Constraint bingo studio: a bingo-like board of visual constraints such as camera angle, material, era, weather, palette, and composition, with image tiles filling completed rows.
3. Prompt relay telephone: a chain of players pass only an image forward; each station reinterprets it, creating a visible evolution trail from object to poster to scene to interface.
4. Variant draft arena: multiple generated options are laid out like collectible draft picks, and players choose parents to breed the next round of visual variants.
5. Scene grammar kit: modular cards for subject, action, environment, lens, lighting, texture, and mood snap together physically to produce one coherent final scene.
6. Scale-shift microscope: one generated object is explored across macro, normal, miniature, and world-size views, with magnifying instruments and miniature figures for comparison.

Make it look like a reusable playbook for creative experiments, with each panel self-explanatory, no brand logos, no famous people, no copyrighted characters, no exact readable typography.
