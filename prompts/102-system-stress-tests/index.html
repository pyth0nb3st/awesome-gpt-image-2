Use case: productivity-visual
Asset type: GPT Image 2 use-case research board, 3:2 landscape contact sheet
Primary request: Create a polished research board titled "GPT-IMAGE-2 USE CASE LAB: SYSTEM STRESS TESTS" showing six NEW practical use cases for image generation and editing that avoid prior themes such as catalog transformation, data posters, onboarding states, localization packaging, restoration, game asset sheets, prompt archaeology, QA spot-the-diff, microbrand kits, learning cards, wayfinding, tabletop props, transparent stickers, aspect ratio relays, safety sketches, menus, style decks, repair manuals, room lighting, legal risk maps, bookmark memory palaces, event poster stress labs, and object aging.
Panel 1 title: "Process Twin" — a factory maintenance workflow visualized as before, during, after, and exception states with the same machine silhouette preserved.
Panel 2 title: "Field Notebook" — messy field notes and specimen sketches transformed into a clean ecological observation plate with readable labels and uncertainty badges.
Panel 3 title: "Policy Theater" — abstract workplace policy paragraphs turned into role-play scene cards showing safe and unsafe examples, no real companies.
Panel 4 title: "Kitchen Mise Map" — a recipe converted into station setup diagrams, timing strips, ingredient prep bowls, and plating thumbnails.
Panel 5 title: "Archive Color Lab" — black-and-white public-domain-style object photos recolored into three historically plausible palettes with evidence swatches.
Panel 6 title: "Failure Mode Atlas" — one everyday device shown across jammed, overheated, cracked, water-exposed, and repaired states with callout tags.
Composition/framing: six equal panels in a clean 3x2 grid, each with a short readable English title, visual evidence artifacts, and a finished sample output.
Style/medium: premium design-research documentation wall, crisp printed boards, realistic desk objects, high detail, professional lighting.
Color: varied accents per panel, neutral workspace base, avoid a one-hue palette.
Text constraints: render only the title plus the six panel titles and tiny simple labels like "OK", "RISK", "FIX", "SOURCE"; no real brands, no logos, no watermark, no political figures, no copyrighted characters.
