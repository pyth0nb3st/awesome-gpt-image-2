Full Prompt

Use case: infographic-diagram / original GPT Image 2 use-case concept
Asset type: public-gallery concept image, 16:9 landscape
Primary request: Create a single continuous "Prompt Kite Wind Tunnel" scene that visualizes fresh GPT Image 2 use cases as wind-tested kites, not as a grid, not as a dashboard, not as a tabletop board.
Scene/backdrop: a bright fictional indoor wind tunnel shaped like a long curved hangar, with transparent airflow ribbons, hanging calibration weights, chalk marks on the floor, and six different handmade kites suspended along one sweeping S-shaped flight path.
Subject: each kite demonstrates a different image-generation play mechanic: subject spar frame, negative-space tail, reference tether knot, style gust ribbon, tiny-caption streamer, multi-format landing hoop. The mechanics should be communicated through physical kite parts, line tension, airflow traces, and landing targets rather than literal UI panels.
Composition/framing: one panoramic continuous scene from left foreground to right background; the viewer follows the S-shaped kite path through the hangar. Use small fictional labels only where useful; avoid dense readable text. No 2x3 panel layout.
Style/medium: polished editorial 3D illustration with tactile craft materials, vellum, bamboo spars, thread, translucent fabric, chalk dust, and soft studio light.
Interaction idea: the image should imply that GPT Image 2 can test prompt balance, reference pull, style drift, legibility in motion, and aspect-ratio landing accuracy through a playful wind-tunnel artifact.
Safety and rights constraints: all objects, labels, institutions, data, and characters are fictional. No real brands, trademarks, celebrities, copyrighted characters, living-artist style imitation, political persuasion, parties, elections, public political figures, sexual content, nudity, harmful instructions, weapons, or graphic violence.
Output constraints: landscape image, high detail, no watermark, no logo, no QR code, no real product names.
