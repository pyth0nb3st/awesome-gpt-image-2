Panel C label: "19. Feed DNA Mutator". Derived from Persona Style Bible. Visual: nine social post thumbnails for the same fictional science educator, varying only framing, prop, density, and emotional tone; include a small scoring strip for recognition vs novelty.
Panel D label: "20. Renovation Constraint Solver". Derived from Before-After Decision Tree. Visual: a room planning board where constraints are movable tokens: budget, renter-safe, video calls, storage, lighting, noise; three floor-plan thumbnails update under different token combinations.
