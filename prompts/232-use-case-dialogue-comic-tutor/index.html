Full Prompt

Use case: scientific-educational
Asset type: public gallery concept image, vertical 4:5 printable classroom worksheet
Primary request: Create one polished concept image titled exactly "Use Case: Dialogue Comic Tutor". Show GPT Image 2 as a safe language-learning workflow that turns a tiny fictional cafe scene into a clear four-panel bilingual practice comic with readable short text and correction checkpoints.

Output format: one vertical 4:5 printable worksheet page, not a physical tabletop object, not a device, not a tray, not a lab bench, not a contact sheet, not a 2x3 research board. It should look like a finished educational handout that could hang on a classroom wall.

Scene/backdrop: clean ivory paper worksheet with four comic panels arranged as a flowing page, plus a narrow right-side checklist column. The comic is set in an imaginary unbranded community cafe called "Blue Cup Room". Use only fictional adult characters: Mara, a barista with short black hair and a green apron, and Niko, a visitor with round glasses and a yellow scarf. Keep both characters consistent across panels.

Panel story: Panel 1, Niko enters and points at the menu. Panel 2, Mara offers tea. Panel 3, a speech bubble is corrected with a small edit mark. Panel 4, both smile at a table with the final corrected dialogue visible.

Exact readable text to render, keep short: title "Use Case: Dialogue Comic Tutor"; panel labels "1", "2", "3", "4"; speech bubbles "Tea?", "Yes", "谢谢", "Thanks"; checklist labels "Text", "Tone", "Face", "Panel", "PASS"; cafe sign "Blue Cup Room". Do not add random placeholder text.

GPT Image play mechanism shown visually: tiny colored check chips next to each panel mark text accuracy, tone consistency, face consistency, panel order, and final pass. Show a small before/after speech bubble in panel 3 where a garbled bubble becomes "谢谢" using a clean edit arrow. The worksheet should make the workflow understandable at a glance without long explanation.

Visual style: contemporary educational comic illustration, warm but not childish, clean ink line, soft watercolor fills, clear white speech bubbles, accessible contrast, generous margins, graphite text, teal and saffron accents, small coral correction mark. Avoid purple-dominant gradients, decorative blobs, fake brand logos, real trademarks, famous characters, celebrity likenesses, political content, adult content, violence, medical/legal claims, dangerous instructions, living-artist style imitation, watermark.
