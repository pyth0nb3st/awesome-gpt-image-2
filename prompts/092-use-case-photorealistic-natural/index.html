Use case: photorealistic-natural
Asset type: exploratory GPT Image 2 use-case card, single finished image
Primary request: Create a 16:9 "SAME ROOM, FOUR HOURS" concept image showing one small reading corner rendered consistently at morning, noon, dusk, and night in four vertical slices. This demonstrates scene consistency, lighting/time variation, and spatial memory without changing furniture.
Scene/backdrop: cozy apartment reading nook with one armchair, side table, plant, bookshelf, window, and mug.
Subject: identical room layout across four time states.
Style/medium: photorealistic interior photography, natural textures.
Composition/framing: one seamless room divided into four vertical time bands, same camera angle.
Lighting/mood: morning soft sun, noon bright daylight, dusk warm orange, night lamp glow.
Color palette: varied by time while furniture stays constant.
Text (verbatim): small unobtrusive labels only: "MORNING", "NOON", "DUSK", "NIGHT".
Constraints: keep furniture positions identical, no people, no logos, no watermark, no fantasy elements.
