Full Prompt

Create a single polished gallery image for a derived play concept titled Derived Play: Onion Skin Relay. It should be a different artifact from the flipbook: show a transparent tabletop relay rig with eight upright frame cards, sliding vellum ghost layers, alignment pegs, caption beat chips, palette freeze clips, crop windows, a tiny retake bell, and continuity tokens beside a thumb-test rail. The gameplay mechanism is cooperative frame repair: players draft one frame card, slide an onion-skin ghost over it, swap exactly one caption beat, freeze one palette swatch, crop one window, and ring the retake bell if the invented clockwork fruit courier changes silhouette or prop shape; the relay wins when the thumb-test rail shows a smooth eight-frame motion. Readable labels should include Frame Draft, Ghost Align, Beat Swap, Palette Freeze, Crop Window, Retake Bell, Flip Test, and Continuity Token.
