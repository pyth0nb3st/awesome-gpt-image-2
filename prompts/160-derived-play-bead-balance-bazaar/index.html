Full Prompt

Visible title: "Derived Play: Bead Balance Bazaar". Create a compact handcrafted tabletop skill game photographed from a low oblique angle, 16:9. It is a semicircular bead market tray with tilting rails, bead bowls, tiny balance scales, felt score cups, and removable square/tall/wide frame tickets. Seven etched rules are visible: Draft Row, Cap Phrase, Trade Weight, Letter Toll, Frame Ticket, Echo Tip, Seal Pot. Play: draft one subject bead row, cap a phrase before it crowds the tray, trade weight beads without tipping the scale, pay a letter toll for tiny wording, pick a frame ticket, correct drift by tipping one rail back, then seal the score pot. No hands or people. It should feel like a new bead economy game, not a normal abacus diagram and not any previous gallery apparatus such as grids, cards, routes, chips, mazes, stages, gardens, kitchens, paper rolls, trays, presses, looms, weather devices, pinball, or kite lines. All content is fictional and rights-safe; avoid unsafe, adult, graphic, political, branded, celebrity, or copyrighted material. No watermark or QR code.
