Full Prompt

Create a second polished GPT Image 2 derived-play board as a single 3:2 landscape image, laid out as a clean 2x3 contact sheet with six distinct panels. This board should derive playful mechanics from the previous use cases, with compact legible English panel labels. No giant title, no logos, no watermarks.

Panel 1 label: AVATAR CONSENT DICE. Show a tabletop-style rules mat with dice for pose, camera angle, outfit coverage, and approval states; the game is about generating only consent-safe avatar variants.
Panel 2 label: ASSEMBLY TIME TRIAL. Show three lamp assembly snapshots as a timed puzzle lane, with ghost overlays, error tokens, and a final alignment score.
Panel 3 label: CURATOR SWITCHBOARD. Show one artifact at the center and toggle cards for audience age, reading level, language tone, and label length; outputs become different museum cards.
Panel 4 label: FIXTURE BOSS RUSH. Show a QA arcade board with synthetic image challenges: glare, blur, occlusion, tiny text, low light, reflection; each has a multimodal test badge.
Panel 5 label: ROOM MOOD SPELLBOOK. Show a recipe-like visual spellbook for transforming a room mood with lighting, object swaps, texture cards, and forbidden style clashes.
Panel 6 label: RULEBOOK MUTATION LAB. Show a controlled mutation lab for a fictional game item: sliders for silhouette, palette, scale, background noise, and icon readability, with approved/rejected outputs.

Style: refined visual design, research-lab meets game manual, tactile paper and screen mockups, warm gray background with varied accent colors, clear panel boundaries, professional composition, no decorative blobs, avoid a one-note purple/blue palette. Keep all content fictional and privacy-safe.
