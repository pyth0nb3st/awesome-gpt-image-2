Lighting/mood: warm studio tabletop lighting, playful but practical, immediately understandable.
Color palette: neutral table, varied accent colors for each game, crisp black rule text, colorful scoring tokens.
Text constraints: render only the overall title, six panel titles, and simple words "PASS", "RISK", "FIX", "SCORE", "ROUND"; no logos, no watermarks, no copyrighted characters.
