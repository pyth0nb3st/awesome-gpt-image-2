Full Prompt

Create one polished public-gallery concept image titled exactly "Derived Play: Wrap Seam Sweep".

Output format: one ultra-wide 2:1 playable panorama QA overlay, full-bleed immersive 360 scene with game rules embedded as short labels. It must not be a physical tabletop object, not a board game photo, not a device, not a tray, not a lab bench, not a map, not a comic, not a worksheet, not a contact sheet, and not a dashboard-first UI. The environment should dominate, with translucent game overlays.

Scene: same fictional no-people Aurora Reading Court, but now shown as a wraparound seam-check challenge. Keep the circular reading courtyard, brass moon gate split across the far left and far right edges, glass ceiling ribs, paper lanterns, shallow reflecting channel, tiled floor compass motif, curved book alcoves, and plants. Make the left and right edges intentionally comparable: matching gate curve, matching lantern pair, matching floor tile line.

Derived gameplay mechanism: players sweep a thin glowing path across the panorama and verify continuity. Add compact overlay labels only: "Derived Play: Wrap Seam Sweep", "Start", "Sweep", "Match", "Light", "Count 5", "Repair", "Wrap OK", "PASS 8". Show five small numbered anchor pips along the horizon and seam windows, but keep numbers tiny and readable: "1", "2", "3", "4", "5". Show one coral repair ribbon near a slight seam warning, then a green pass chip at the opposite edge.

Composition: seamless 2:1 panorama feeling, horizon centered, full-bleed immersive environment, no people, no UI cards, no dense paragraphs. The game overlay should look like transparent acetate on a 360 preview: hairline guides, small chips, one continuous sweep path, edge comparison brackets, and anchor pips. Use warm amber lantern light, deep teal shadow, graphite guide lines, ivory text, small coral repair mark, green pass marks.

Safety and rights: fictional location and fictional game only, no real brands, no trademarks, no public figures, no politics, no elections, no dangerous instructions, no adult or explicit content, no gore or violence, no copyrighted characters, no living-artist style imitation, no source image reuse, no watermark.
