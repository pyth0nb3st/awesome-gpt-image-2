Overall tiny header: "GPT-IMAGE-2 USE CASE LAB: MATERIAL + ACCESSIBILITY PLAY". Each panel has a short readable English label and shows a fictional, practical image-generation workflow:
1 "Tactile Diagram Draft" - a fictional kitchen timer and plant pot transformed into high-contrast raised-line style diagrams with braille-like dotted texture placeholders, accessibility education concept.

3 "Season Wear Simulator" - one generic outdoor stool shown across sun, rain, frost, and dust aging previews, material planning concept.
4 "Cardboard Kit Forge" - a rough sketch of a small imaginary desk organizer turned into punch-out cardboard parts and assembled prototype steps.
5 "Mascot Mood Notes" - abstract daily moods converted into friendly non-human geometric mascots on journaling cards, no therapy claims.
6 "Fictional Exhibit Mini-Tours" - a invented object collection turned into small visitor route thumbnails and plain-language captions, all fictional.
