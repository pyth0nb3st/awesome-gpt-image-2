Full Prompt

Derived Play: Texture Cipher Duet. Make one square 1:1 overhead product photograph of two concentric charcoal felt dials side by side, linked by a brass bridge. Each dial has twelve shallow velvet pockets, pearl raised-dot tiles, crescent ridge tiles, texture swatches, brass privacy shutters, weight beads, label tabs, and a central matching aperture. One player secretly sets a Seed Tile under the left shutter; the other rotates the Texture Dial, swaps one Ridge Tile, spends one Weight Bead, closes a Decoy Shutter, names the Caption Tab, and aligns the Aperture Pair. A round scores at the Feel Match bowl only when both dials reveal the same invented opal seed comma: three raised pearl dots plus one crescent ridge. Keep it fictional, no-people, unbranded, harmless, not a real tactile writing system, not a relay, not a path lane, not a proof tunnel, not a grid, not a contact sheet, not a music box cylinder, not an abacus, and not a proof press.
