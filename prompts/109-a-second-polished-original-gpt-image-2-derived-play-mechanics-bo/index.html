Full Prompt

Create a second polished original GPT Image 2 derived-play mechanics board as a single 3:2 landscape image, clean 2x3 contact-sheet layout with six clearly separated panels. No logos, no real brands, no celebrity likenesses, no public figures, no copyrighted characters, no politics, no sexual content, no violence, no watermarks. Overall tiny header: "GPT-IMAGE-2 DERIVED PLAY MODES: MATERIAL ARCADE". Derive playful, recordable image-generation games from the prior material/accessibility use cases. Each panel has a short readable English label and looks like a complete tabletop or studio challenge card:
1 "Texture Cipher Relay" - players convert everyday object photos into raised-line texture keys, then another player reconstructs the object category from the diagram.
2 "Drawer Tetris Draft" - randomized fictional tools must fit into a foam insert grid; show tokens, grid slots, and scored layout cards.
3 "Weather Oracle Cards" - material sample cards are dealt with sun/rain/frost/dust modifiers to predict visible aging outcomes.
4 "Prototype Punch-Out Duel" - two paper-kit designs compete through constraints: fewest folds, strongest shape, smallest scrap pile.
5 "Mood Mascot Mixer" - emotion words, color chips, and shape dice combine into non-human mascot families with consistency rules.
6 "Mini-Tour Stamp Rally" - fictional exhibit objects become route stamps; players build the clearest three-stop visitor path from shuffled object cards.
Style: collectible creative game-design spread, crisp typography, realistic cards and tokens photographed on a clean studio table, thin dividers, restrained varied accent colors, legible labels, professional editorial design, no brand marks.
