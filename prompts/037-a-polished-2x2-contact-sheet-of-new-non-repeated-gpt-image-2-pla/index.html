Full Prompt

Create a polished 2x2 contact sheet of NEW, non-repeated GPT Image 2 playground use cases. Do not depict generic ads, UI dashboards, infocards, comics, product kits, localization posters, visual quizzes, tabletop games, children's books, creator shot recipes, thumbnail ladders, maps, receipts, calendars, or worldbuilding kits. Overall style: premium applied-research board, clean margins, inspectable details, no real brands, no watermarks.

Panel A label: "13. Service Rehearsal". Visual: a split-scene simulation of a small clinic reception during a rush hour, showing staff positions, customer emotions, queue friction points, accessibility concerns, and service recovery moments as realistic annotated storyboard stills.
Panel B label: "14. Architecture X-Ray". Visual: a software architecture concept translated into a layered physical cutaway machine: API gateway, worker queue, cache, database, observability pipes, failure points as tangible components, readable labels only for these system parts.
Panel C label: "15. Persona Style Bible". Visual: a social account visual personality system for a fictional science educator: profile avatar concepts, recurring framing rules, color accents, prop vocabulary, caption tone cards, without platform logos.
Panel D label: "16. Before-After Decision Tree". Visual: a home office improvement board that branches from one messy room into three realistic redesign paths: budget fix, ergonomic fix, creator studio fix; include material samples and tradeoff notes.

Use case: productivity-visual
Asset type: non-repeated use-case contact sheet
Primary request: draw a new group of GPT Image 2 use cases that avoid prior recorded directions and focus on service simulation, code architecture visualization, creator visual systems, and decision-tree planning.
Composition: 2x2 grid, each panel distinct and useful, crisp labels, realistic production quality.
Avoid: previously recorded concepts, real company branding, celebrity likenesses, copyrighted characters, illegible gibberish, decorative-only visuals.
