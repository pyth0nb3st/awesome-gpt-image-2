Full Prompt

Create one polished public-gallery image titled "Derived Play: Dew Trace Curling". It is derived from Prompt Condensation Chamber but becomes a different playable object: a vertical 4:5 overhead-oblique product photograph of a circular matte-black tabletop curling arena with three transparent dew lanes spiraling inward, six chilled glass pucks, brass score calipers, soft eraser sleeves, fog tokens, a mint-lit holding bell, and one pearlescent violet comma comet marker with two brass fins and one mint dot. Play rules shown through pieces: slide a Dew Puck through a spiral lane, spend a Fog Token to hide part of the trail, rotate one Vane Turn piece, place an Erase Sleeve to remove a wrong curl, write one tiny word on the Word Strip, clip a Frost Arc to change the scoring window, then score Trace Hold only if the holding bell still shows the same violet comma comet and the condensation trail lands inside the Caliper Score. Visible labels: Dew Puck, Fog Token, Vane Turn, Erase Sleeve, Word Strip, Frost Arc, Hold Bell, Caliper Score. No people, no hands, no logos, no real sports branding, no real lab equipment. Premium tactile tabletop prototype, cool mist, clear glass, brass hardware, mint accent light, crisp readable labels; fictional, original, unbranded, harmless, rights-safe.
