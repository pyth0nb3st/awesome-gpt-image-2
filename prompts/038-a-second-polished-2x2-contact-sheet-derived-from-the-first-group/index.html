Create a second polished 2x2 contact sheet derived from the first group's ideas, exploring playful variations of GPT Image 2 workflows. Overall style: premium experimental design board, clean margins, consistent labels, no brand logos, no watermarks.

Panel A label: "5. Recipe card remix". Visual: a vertical recipe card generated from the noodle poster concept, with an illustrated bowl, ingredient icons, and readable exact title "Midnight Noodle Kit" plus short clean steps.
Panel B label: "6. Dashboard to room". Visual: transform the finance dashboard idea into a physical command-center desk setup: monitor shows neat widgets, sticky notes, calendar blocks, readable labels "Budget", "Focus", "Save".
Panel C label: "7. Character sheet". Visual: the same red delivery robot from the comic as a game-ready character sheet: front, side, back, expressions, prop teacup, color swatches, compact notes.
Panel D label: "8. Ad campaign triptych". Visual: three small product-ad variations for the translucent folding desk lamp: home office, student dorm, maker bench; each with distinct lighting and readable headlines "Clear Light", "Fold Flat", "Build Bright".

Use case: productivity-visual
Asset type: derived-play contact sheet
Primary request: derive new gameplay from the first group: remix text-heavy poster into recipe content, turn UI into physical scene, turn sequential character into reusable asset sheet, turn product render into campaign variants.
Composition: 2x2 grid, each panel visually distinct but coherent, production-grade, inspectable details.
Avoid: real brands, celebrity likenesses, copyrighted characters, gibberish labels, excessive tiny text, cluttered layout.
