Use case: stylized-concept
Asset type: Drill website landing page hero image, wide 16:9 marketing visual
Primary request: Create a bold abstract brand visual for Drill, an AI reading comprehension product that lets users drill into anything they read.
Scene/backdrop: a dark-ink editorial page or dense paragraph dissolving into a luminous branching knowledge tree, with subtle interface layers and depth cues emerging from highlighted phrases.
Subject: the transformation from confusing text into structured understanding, shown as glowing recursive layers expanding out of a single highlighted sentence.
Style/medium: premium product-brand illustration, cinematic 3D-meets-editorial, polished startup marketing aesthetic, distinctive not generic, high contrast, refined detail.
Composition/framing: wide composition with strong negative space on the right for headline and CTA overlay, focal energy on the left-center, clean readable silhouette.
Lighting/mood: intelligent, exploratory, precise, calm but powerful; soft volumetric glow from the knowledge tree.
Color palette: off-white paper tones, deep graphite, electric cyan, subtle teal, restrained warm highlights.
Materials/textures: paper grain, glassy UI layers, soft light bloom, crisp typographic-texture hints without readable text.
Constraints: no readable text, no logos, no watermark, no clutter, no purple-dominant palette, suitable for SaaS marketing hero use.
