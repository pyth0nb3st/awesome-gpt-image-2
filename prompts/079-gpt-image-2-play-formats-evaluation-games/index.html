Full Prompt

Create a second single high-quality landscape concept board showing six NEW derived game-like GPT Image 2 play formats inspired by the previous use cases. Avoid repeating all previous play formats: hidden-object hunt, brand slot machine, moodboard battle, time-machine photo chain, character passport, prompt recipe kitchen, toy remixes, micro-brand kits, old photo repair, regression detective, constraint bingo, prompt relay telephone, variant draft arena, scene grammar kit, scale-shift microscope, plus the six use cases from the previous board. 

No readable text except tiny abstract marks. Style: polished tabletop/game design artifact plus creative operations studio, six-panel grid, energetic but organized, clear interaction mechanics, balanced multi-color palette, no logos, no famous people, no copyrighted characters.

Six fresh derived play formats:
1. Continuity wardrobe audit: players receive generated outfit boards and score whether fabric, wear, season logic, and packing constraints stay consistent across image rounds.
2. Gallery layout speedrun: players race to arrange generated mini-exhibits under constraints like visitor path, lighting budget, object fragility, and wall density.
3. Drill-path puzzle: generated floor plans and scene cards become a cooperative route-planning puzzle where players place blockers, exits, and assembly markers.
4. Material truth table: one object is generated in many materials; players rank which version best communicates weight, softness, durability, cost, and repairability.
5. Waveform stage jam: players convert abstract sound cards into stage scene prompts, then compare the resulting lighting, props, and movement language.
6. Maintenance triage draft: generated machine-inspection cards are drafted into urgent, watch-list, and safe piles based on visible wear, residue, missing parts, and tool evidence.

Make it look like a reusable play kit for evaluating and playing with image generation, with each panel self-explanatory visually.
