Full Prompt

Create a single high-resolution 3-panel derived-play concept board. Three vertical panels side by side, separated by thin grid lines. These are playful derivations from the previous three concepts, not the same images.

Panel 1: Poster-to-AR installation. The "月光档案馆 / MOONLIGHT ARCHIVE" event poster has become an immersive street AR wayfinding scene. A person holds a phone in front of the poster, and the phone screen shows glowing moonlit archive shelves extending into the real street with elegant arrows and tiny floating labels. Keep the bilingual event identity visible but not dominant. Cinematic night photography, plausible UI overlay, legible key words only.

Panel 2: Cutaway-to-learning-kit. The ramen vending machine cutaway has been transformed into a tactile STEM learning kit on a classroom table: magnetic machine layers, labeled cards, tiny pump/valve tokens, a fold-out instruction mat, and kids' hands assembling it. Labels on cards in Chinese: "汤泵", "面仓", "蒸汽阀", "调味轮". Warm documentary product photography, organized parts, educational but fun.

Panel 3: Character-to-merch system. The Lumo moon courier mascot becomes a cohesive merchandise launch board: sticker sheet, enamel pin, tote bag, small plush, postcard, and shipping-box tape. Maintain the same moon-shaped courier with blue scarf and satchel. Include small clean brand text "Lumo Courier" on packaging only. Bright studio flat lay, consistent brand colors, realistic materials.

Overall: polished concept research board, high detail, no watermark, no clutter, keep all text minimal and as specified, avoid copyrighted styles or characters.
