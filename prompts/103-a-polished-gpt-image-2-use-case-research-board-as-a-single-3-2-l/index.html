Full Prompt

Create a polished GPT-image-2 use-case research board as a single 3:2 landscape image, like a high-end design lab documentation artifact. Six clearly separated panels in a 3x2 grid, each panel with a short readable English title and a distinct visual demo. Overall title: "GPT-Image-2 Use Case Lab: Evidence to Systems".

Panel 1 title: "Field Note Builder" showing messy handwritten field notes transformed into a clean illustrated observation sheet with icons, map pins, specimen thumbnails, and a dated log strip.
Panel 2 title: "Constraint Posterizer" showing one plain prompt becoming three poster variants under visible constraints: low ink, high contrast, and one-object-only.
Panel 3 title: "Recipe Reality Check" showing a fictional recipe card converted into plated food, ingredient layout, timing diagram, and allergy badge system.
Panel 4 title: "Policy Comic Translator" showing a dense workplace policy paragraph transformed into a four-panel comic explainer with clear visual steps and readable labels.
Panel 5 title: "Archive Label Restorer" showing damaged museum object labels reconstructed into plausible clean catalog cards with accession codes, era tags, and object thumbnails.
Panel 6 title: "Failure Mode Catalog" showing a generated product image audit wall: warped text, missing shadow, wrong scale, repeated object, and corrected examples.

Style: premium editorial collage, crisp realistic miniatures mixed with clean interface-like labels, varied colors across panels, professional lighting, sharp details.
Text constraints: render only the overall title and the six panel titles exactly as listed plus tiny simple labels where needed; avoid long unreadable paragraphs; no logos, no brands, no watermark.
Composition: six equal panels in a clean 3x2 grid, balanced margins, print-quality contact sheet.
