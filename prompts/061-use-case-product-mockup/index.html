Use case: product-mockup
Asset type: product concept sheet, 16:9 landscape
Primary request: Create a compact product-visualization board for a fictional portable creative tool called "Pocket Studio".
Scene/backdrop: bright tabletop studio with three realistic product angles.
Subject: a small handheld matte white device with an e-ink screen, side dial, USB-C port, and modular color shells; show front, side, and in-use angle.
Text: include exactly these labels: "Pocket Studio", "Shell swap", "Prompt dial", "USB-C".
Composition: catalog mockup layout with callout lines, color swatches, subtle shadows, very clean spacing.
Style: premium product render, realistic materials, editorial industrial design board.
Avoid: real brand logos, unreadable labels, excessive futuristic shapes, watermark.
