Full Prompt

Create a polished 2x2 contact sheet titled "GPT Image 2: System Play Use Cases". Each panel should look like a finished concept mockup, with short crisp English labels only. Use a clean editorial design, high fidelity, realistic printed materials mixed with illustration, no brand logos, no watermarks.

Panel 1 label: "BRANCHING PICTURE BOOK". Show an open children's picture book spread with a friendly original character in a cozy workshop, plus exactly three visible illustrated choice cards at the bottom labeled "Door", "Map", "Song". Include tiny next-scene thumbnails connected to those choices.

Panel 2 label: "CREATOR SHOT RECIPE". Show a short-video planning board on a desk: hook frame, prop setup, overhead camera position diagram, caption overlay sample, and thumbnail sketch. It should feel useful for a YouTube/TikTok creator planning a shoot today.

Panel 3 label: "VISUAL EXAM BANK". Show four educational quiz cards with small science diagrams, A/B/C choices, and a discreet answer-key strip. Keep text short and legible: "Force", "Heat", "Orbit", "Signal".

Panel 4 label: "TABLETOP PROTOTYPE KIT". Show a print-and-play board game prototype kit: cards, tokens, player mat, rule reminder, and box-front mockup on a neutral tabletop. Use visible numbers and icons, but keep all text short and clean.

Overall: four distinct panels, consistent margins, sharp readable headings, playful but professional, 16:9 landscape.
