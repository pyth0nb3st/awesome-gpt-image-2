Full Prompt

Create a polished landscape 16:9 concept image for a derived GPT Image 2 play mode.

Title visible in the artwork: "DRIFT BINGO REVIEW".

Scene: a full-screen collaborative review surface, like a design team critique wall, showing the generated "Lumen Lane Book Swap" SOP document as the large central artifact. Around it are playful but professional bingo-style review overlays, not physical cards: translucent squares and stickers labeled "MISSPELL", "STEP GAP", "ROLE DRIFT", "TINY TEXT", "CLUTTER", "ACCESS", "PASS". A left rail shows three review rounds: "1 OCR", "2 ORDER", "3 HUMAN". A bottom status bar says "FIND 5 DRIFTS BEFORE APPROVAL" and a green stamp reads "PASS AFTER REPAIR".

Mechanism to make visible: reviewers use the game to catch plausible-but-wrong visual generation failures: garbled words, sequence errors, mismatched roles, inaccessible contrast, and generic AI polish. Show exactly five marked drift findings with small arrows, and two repaired findings crossed off. Keep the SOP readable enough to understand it is a safe fictional community book-swap setup guide, but this image is about review gameplay rather than making another instruction sheet.

Visual style: refined product-design critique board, warm off-white background, charcoal UI lines, muted teal, coral, yellow, and green badges, small human annotation marks for authenticity, clean hierarchy, no logos, no real brands, no real people, no copyrighted characters, no politics, no harmful instructions, no gore, no explicit content. Avoid tabletop game photography, avoid a simple 2x3 contact sheet, avoid maps, avoid comics, avoid lab apparatuses, avoid panorama seam QA.
