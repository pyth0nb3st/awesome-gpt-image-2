Full Prompt

Create a single gallery image titled Derived Play: Notch Fitting Swap. Show a curved miniature dress-form stand used as a magnetic fitting game, not a flat board, not cards, not a grid, not a contact sheet. Players would place paper pattern pieces around the form: choose one grain pick, close two notch locks, swap one lining, ban one seam, set a hem crop ruler, attach a stitch read tag, adjust a dart scale clip, and obey a bolt limit roll. The win condition is three fit proof tickets preserving the same pocket-size amber compass across applique, label, and icon. Labels: Grain Pick, Notch Lock, Lining Swap, Seam Ban, Hem Crop, Stitch Read, Dart Scale, Bolt Limit, Fit Proof.
