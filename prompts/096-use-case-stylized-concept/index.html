Use case: stylized-concept
Asset type: GPT Image 2 gallery research board, 2K landscape contact sheet
Primary request: Create a polished 3x2 visual research board showing six NEW practical and playful GPT Image 2 use cases based on current Images 2.0 capabilities: stronger instruction following, dense in-image text, high-fidelity image inputs, flexible aspect ratios, transparent/detail-oriented image edits, multi-output generation, and thinking-mode planning. Avoid repeating prior gallery themes such as repair manuals, museum wayfinding, continuity prop bibles, visual QA rubrics, same-room lighting, receipt-to-menu worlds, research figure remixers, interior swaps, policy ad labs, accessibility simulators, script blocking, future wear forecasts, flavor atlases, synthetic dataset gap fillers, board-game conversions, prompt relay, constraint bingo, regression detective, claim court, director ladder, and signage escape rooms.
Panels:
1. Live Fact Card Forge: a research desk where a model plans a fresh web-informed infographic card, with date stamps, source tabs, thumbnail map, and a final concise explainer card.
2. Transparent Sticker Factory: a creator table making clean cutout sticker sheets from ordinary objects, with alpha checkerboard previews, edge inspection loupe, and export tiles.
3. Aspect Ratio Shape-Shifter: one campaign concept shown as horizontal banner, square post, vertical story, and ultra-tall poster while preserving the same character, prop, and message.
4. Sketch-to-Safety Prototype: a rough napkin sketch of a household gadget becomes three safe product mockups with rounded edges, warning icons, and material callouts.
5. Multilingual Tiny-Type Menu: a compact fictional cafe board with readable English, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, and Arabic labels arranged in dense but clean typography.
6. Multi-Output Style Deck: eight coordinated visual directions generated from one brief, displayed as a selector board with style chips, risk notes, and pick markers.
Style/medium: premium design-research wall, realistic printed boards, screens, cutout previews, prompt cards, thumbnails, annotation stickers, no real brands.
Composition/framing: 3 columns by 2 rows, each panel visually distinct with a short simple title; visible before-to-after or source-to-output relationship in every panel.
Lighting/mood: bright studio documentation lighting, crisp and inventive, production-ready rather than whimsical.
Color palette: neutral workspace, black ink, teal/amber/red/blue accent systems, varied per panel.
Text (verbatim, keep simple and legible where possible): "FACT CARD", "TRANSPARENT", "WIDE", "SQUARE", "VERTICAL", "SAFE MOCKUP", "MENU", "STYLE DECK".
Constraints: no logos, no watermarks, no real company marks, no copyrighted characters, no real news claims, no political figures; make every panel feel like a recordable use case with components and evidence.
