Full Prompt

Create a polished GPT-image-2 use-case research board as a single 3:2 landscape image, like a high-end editorial design research artifact. Six clearly separated panels in a 3x2 grid, each panel with a short readable English title and a distinct visual demo. Overall title: "GPT-Image-2 Use Case Lab: Batch 2026-04-25 A". Panel 1 title: "Catalog Transformer" showing one product silhouette becoming four catalog-ready product photos with different materials. Panel 2 title: "Data-to-Poster" showing a small chart and numbers transformed into a clean infographic poster. Panel 3 title: "Onboarding States" showing mobile app screens for empty, loading, success, and error states. Panel 4 title: "Localized Shelf" showing the same fictional snack packaging localized for English, Japanese, and Arabic with accurate-looking layout diversity, no real brands. Panel 5 title: "Restoration What-if" showing an old damaged storefront photo reconstructed into plausible restored facades across eras. Panel 6 title: "Game Asset Sheet" showing a consistent fantasy tool kit with icons, item cards, and a small inventory UI. Crisp typography, no logos, no watermarks, rich but restrained color, sharp details, professional gallery-ready composition.
